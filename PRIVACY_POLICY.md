# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 13, 2026**

## Overview

Goaly ("the App") is a Pomodoro timer and task management application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

## Data Collection

**Goaly does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

All data created within the App (tasks, tags, settings, time tracking) is stored locally on your device only. No data is sent to external servers, analytics services, or third parties.

## Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device:
- Tasks and task descriptions
- Tags and tag assignments
- Timer settings and preferences
- Time tracking statistics
- Theme preferences

This data remains on your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

## Backup & Export

The App includes an optional export feature that saves your data as a JSON file to a location you choose. This file remains under your control and is not transmitted anywhere automatically.

## Notifications

The App uses local notifications to alert you when timers complete. These notifications are processed entirely on your device and do not involve any external services.

## Third-Party Services

Goaly does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

Goaly does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
